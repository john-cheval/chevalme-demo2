Our Process

Discovery Phase

Discovery is the important 1st phase of the web development life cycle. It involves thorough research, gathering of information, and collaborative efforts to establish the scope, feature requirements, stakeholders, processes, and project expectations. In the Discovery phase, the website design team in Abu Dhabi delves deeper into each feature to confirm their scope and uncover any additional requirements in the web design paper.

Wireframe Design

Once the web page templates that need to be built have been identified, the next step is to create wireframes. Wireframes are simple sketches that show how a page template will be structured. Using black and white elements, wireframes visually represent where page components will be placed. This allows users to evaluate the layout and make any necessary adjustments.

Web Design Phase

In this phase, the designers utilize colors, fonts, styling, images, callouts, sizing, and layouts. Web design brings your website to life and provides you with a realistic preview of how the live site will look. In addition to aesthetics, web design also considers the mobile and SEO aspects to ensure that the new site is appropriately laid out for users on all devices and structured in an easily understood way by search engines.

Website Development
